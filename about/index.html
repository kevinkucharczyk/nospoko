Nospoko, founded in 2016 by Starzec, is a premier software company specializing in advanced analytics and machine learning. The core competences of computer science, mathematics and physics are applied to develop remarkable software solutions in medical informatics, applied scientific computation and industry.

Nospoko aims to be a creative space where Polish scientists can carry out research without the burden of Polish academia bureaucracy. We incorporate computer science, physics and mathematics with practical experience and thereby develop individual software solutions for companies, medicine and industry.

Our interdisciplinary team is composed of highly-skilled, dedicated professional programmers, analysts, and scientists who are committed to providing innovative solutions to any ‘hard-to-solve’ problem.

Projects we are currently working on

  • automation of medical data analysis
  • software interfaces supporting mathematical pen-on-paper calculations
  • emergent nature of Quantum Mechanics
  • musical AI trying to learn secret trades of Progressive Metal
  • ground rules for esthetically pleasing wedlock of Art and Science
